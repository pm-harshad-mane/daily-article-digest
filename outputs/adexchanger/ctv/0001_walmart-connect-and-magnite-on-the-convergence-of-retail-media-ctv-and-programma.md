## Summary

Walmart Connect and Magnite are driving the convergence of retail media, Connected TV (CTV), and programmatic advertising by integrating Walmart's sizable retail customer base and physical store footprint with Magnite’s premium programmatic CTV inventory. This collaboration aims to move away from fragmented or short-term advertising solutions toward unified, omni-channel marketing strategies that connect brand storytelling with performance, including measurement of offline sales outcomes.

## Problem / Workflow Challenge

The main challenge addressed is the historical fragmentation between retail media, CTV, and programmatic ecosystems, which has created difficulties in delivering seamless advertising experiences that balance brand engagement with direct performance metrics. Retailers and advertisers struggle with disjointed workflows where retail media and CTV operate in silos, limiting holistic measurement and durable growth.

## Solution / Key Development

Walmart Connect and Magnite’s partnership provides a unified ecosystem combining Walmart’s extensive consumer data and physical assets with Magnite’s advanced programmatic CTV capabilities. This integration enables omnichannel advertising campaigns that are measurable through all stages of the consumer journey—including online impressions and offline sales impact—thus offering advertisers durable growth and deeper insight. The collaboration focuses on eliminating short-term fixes and delivering a more robust, long-term solution for programmatic retail media and CTV convergence.

## Why This Matters for My Work

As a Principal Architect at an SSP, understanding this convergence informs how to architect platforms that support unified data integration and seamless execution across retail media and CTV channels. This partnership exemplifies the trend toward omni-channel programmatic ecosystems where identity resolution, cross-device measurement (online to offline), and premium inventory access are central. It underscores the need for SSPs to adapt to retail media demands, strengthen CTV integrations, and support durable, measurable advertising workflows that deliver value to both publishers and advertisers.
