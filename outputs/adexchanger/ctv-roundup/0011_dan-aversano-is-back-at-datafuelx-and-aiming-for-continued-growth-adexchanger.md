## Summary

DatafuelX, a predictive analytics startup focused on optimizing TV and digital media value, has rehired co-founder Dan Aversano as CEO to lead its next growth phase centered on connected TV (CTV). Founded in 2021 with roots in data-driven linear TV, the company aims to expand its technology—specifically its M3 yield optimization platform and PrecisionX ad exposure forecasting tool—into digital and programmatic environments. DatafuelX plans major DSP partnerships to integrate linear ad plans with digital buying, enabling incremental reach and improved targeting across channels. The company remains publisher-focused but is pursuing buy-side benefits and open to SSP collaboration, emphasizing supply-path optimization as a challenge in the multi-SSP publisher landscape.

## Problem / Workflow Challenge

- The ongoing convergence of streaming and traditional TV creates challenges in forecasting demand and targeting across linear and digital channels.
- Disconnect between data science capabilities and deep industry/media strategy understanding results in long, costly learning curves for data teams.
- Media buyers lack tools to unify linear and digital planning, risking audience duplication and inefficient incremental reach.
- Publishers struggle with fragmented inventory due to multiple SSPs, complicating supply-path optimization.
- A large portion of media decision-making still relies on Excel, limiting efficiency and optimization potential.

## Solution / Key Development

- DatafuelX leverages its data forecasting and analytics expertise to optimize convergent media buys across live linear, CTV, FAST, and online video.
- M3 platform enhances yield optimization for publishers and helps aggregate inventory forecasting.
- PrecisionX analytics tool predicts ad exposures at a granular level using digital IDs linked to historical viewing data, showing >80% accuracy in NBCUniversal proof of concept.
- Planned partnerships with DSPs will enable seamless integration of linear ad plans into digital programmatic buys, providing advanced targeting and incremental reach capabilities.
- Openness to working with SSPs to address supply-path optimization challenges, aiming to better consolidate publisher inventory and increase yield.

## Why This Matters for My Work

- The integration of linear and digital TV ad planning is crucial amid the blurring of CTV and traditional TV ecosystems, requiring SSPs to support multi-channel inventory aggregation and forecasting.
- DatafuelX’s PrecisionX provides a significant advance in exposure accuracy, improving measurement and targeting precision for publishers and buyers.
- Planned DSP integrations indicate a shift toward more sophisticated programmatic workflows combining linear and CTV data, impacting auction dynamics, yield optimization, and identity usage.
- Supply-path optimization remains a critical SSP responsibility as multiple SSPs service publisher inventory, making coordination and consolidation key to maximizing monetization.
- Understanding emerging analytics tools and partnerships will help architects design systems that support cross-platform media decisioning and programmatic guaranteed/private marketplace deals.
