## Summary
K-beauty brand Sulwhasoo is accelerating growth in the US by merging upper and lower marketing funnel activities. To attract new customers and drive sales, the brand is combining connected TV (CTV) and digital out-of-home (DOOH) advertising with digital retargeting across desktop and mobile. Using data-driven audience segmentation and exclusion of past purchasers, the campaigns target potential luxury skincare buyers and leverage streaming platforms and digital billboards near retail locations. Sulwhasoo’s efforts show promising engagement and incremental lifts in awareness, purchase intent, foot traffic, and in-store sales.

## Problem / Workflow Challenge
Sulwhasoo faces the challenge of growing its US customer base in a saturated K-beauty market while balancing brand awareness with direct response performance. Measuring incremental conversions is complicated by multiple sales channels (direct-to-consumer and third-party retailers like Amazon and Sephora) where customer purchase data is fragmented or siloed. Additionally, quantifying the direct impact of DOOH campaigns on foot traffic and in-store sales is difficult due to external variables like retail events.

## Solution / Key Development
Sulwhasoo partnered with video ad platform VDX.tv to:
- Launch CTV-first campaigns targeting luxury skincare buyers using robust audience segments compiled from multiple data sources (Foursquare, TransUnion Commerce Signals, Stirista).
- Use QR-coded creatives linking to microsites supporting multi-retailer purchasing.
- Employ retargeting on desktop and mobile with interactive ads to drive engagement and conversions.
- Integrate DOOH near key retail locations with geofencing to target shoppers on the move, ultimately complementing digital efforts.
- Track engagement and incremental metrics including interaction rates (6.5% reported) and awareness lifts (4.1%) using partners like Cint.
- Collaborate with retailers (e.g., Sephora) by sharing conversion pixels to improve cross-platform measurement.

This integrated funnel-collapsing approach bridges upper-funnel brand building with lower-funnel acquisition to sustain growth.

## Why This Matters for My Work
- **Auction & Identity**: Shows advanced audience segmentation using third-party shopper and social data, plus exclusion tactics based on first-party customer records, critical for identity resolution and targeting strategies in programmatic environments.  
- **CTV & DOOH**: Demonstrates how CTV and DOOH can be combined with retargeting to collapse the marketing funnel and boost campaign efficacy—a key trend in omnichannel programmatic.  
- **Measurement & Clean Rooms**: Highlights the challenges SSPs and advertisers face in multi-retailer attribution and the importance of integrations and data sharing (e.g., conversion pixels, possibly clean room collaborations) for accurate measurement.  
- **Publisher Monetization**: Indicates growth potential in premium video inventory and DOOH for brand-driven campaigns, underscoring the value of these channels in SSP monetization roadmaps.  
- **Privacy & Data Use**: Integration of geofencing and cross-device targeting within privacy guardrails is implied, relevant for SSP architecture in managing consent and data hygiene.  
- **Retail Media**: The example of driving foot traffic and sales near retail stores via programmatic DOOH and CTV expands the notion of retail media beyond e-commerce, representing growth opportunities for SSP partnerships.
