## Summary

Magnite has integrated Truthset’s Data Rated Audience into its SSP platform to improve data accuracy and match rates across CTV, mobile, and open web inventory. This integration aims to address the well-known problem of data degradation as IDs move through multiple platforms in the programmatic supply chain. Truthset provides independent accuracy scores on third-party data sets, allowing buyers and sellers to identify and prioritize higher-quality audience data. The partnership enables agencies like PMG to more effectively activate audiences with improved confidence in data quality, particularly important for fragmented CTV environments.

## Problem / Workflow Challenge

- Data accuracy deteriorates significantly as audience IDs hop between platforms, causing loss and errors that reduce match rates.
- Buyers struggle to assess and verify the quality of audience data in their workflow, leading to inefficient targeting and wasted spend.
- CTV inventory is notably fragmented, creating isolated “islands” of data and complicating audience match and activation at scale.
- Current approaches incentivize scale over precision, undermining optimal media spend and campaign performance.

## Solution / Key Development

- Magnite’s integration with Truthset brings Data Rated Audience directly into the SSP, giving immediate access to independently verified data accuracy ratings.
- Truthset aggregates and scores third-party data sets from sources like Experian, Alliant, MRI-Simmons, and TransUnion based on accuracy.
- This data quality layer helps buyers build accuracy checks into their workflows, enabling smarter audience activation and reducing errors.
- The model encourages pricing and selling data based on quality tiers (super premium to inexpensive), optimizing yield and reducing inefficiencies.
- Agencies like PMG leverage this integration alongside their AI analytics platform Alli for cross-channel audience evaluation without managing principles or identity stacks themselves.

## Why This Matters for My Work

- As a Principal Architect at an SSP, integrating independent data quality verification can greatly enhance the value proposition of your CTV and open web inventory by improving audience match accuracy.
- This approach supports better auction outcomes by reducing mismatches and wasted bids due to inaccurate IDs, increasing yield for publishers.
- Aligns with industry trends demanding transparency and quality assurance around identity and audience targeting, critical in a privacy-sensitive environment.
- Enables SSPs to offer data tiering strategies that allow buyers to balance cost versus precision, facilitating smarter retail media and programmatic campaigns.
- The focus on CTV fragmentation and data islands is particularly relevant for designing scalable, interoperable SSP solutions that unify diverse inventory sources.
- Integrations like this reduce the need for SSPs or buyers to build complex proprietary identity solutions, lowering operational complexity.
- Provides groundwork for improved measurement and clean room data validation capabilities by establishing a “source of truth” for audience identity accuracy.
