## Summary

Omnicom announced enhanced capabilities to its cross-platform clean room, enabling measurement of ad reach, frequency, and performance across multiple CTV and linear TV platforms within a single clean room environment. This development addresses the common problem of repetitive ad exposure across streaming platforms by providing granular insights into how often specific creative is served across various publishers. The solution leverages Omnicom’s Acxiom identity infrastructure, in partnership with Snowflake, Albertsons Media Collective, and VideoAmp, allowing advertisers to connect ad frequency data with business outcomes and optimize future campaigns accordingly.

## Problem / Workflow Challenge

- Repetitive ad frequency on CTV platforms causes viewer frustration and potential brand damage.
- Streaming platforms traditionally do not share granular data on ad exposure, making cross-platform frequency capping and performance measurement difficult.
- Advertisers have limited visibility into how often individual households see specific ads across different publishers.
- Average or aggregate reporting obscures the true user-level ad experience, impairing effective optimization.
- Frequency capping issues can lead to “negative reach,” where overexposure hurts brand reputation yet is blamed on the advertiser rather than the platform.

## Solution / Key Development

- Omnicom’s updated cross-platform clean room enables post-campaign measurement of reach, frequency, and performance of campaigns across major streaming (Disney, NBCUniversal, Paramount) and linear TV broadcasters.
- Incorporates a Creative ID embedded in ad creatives to track frequency per platform.
- Uses Acxiom’s Real ID to map household-level ad exposures and link them with conversions or sales.
- Partners with Snowflake (cloud infrastructure), Albertsons Media Collective (retail media platform), and VideoAmp (video measurement with ACR and set-top-box data) to enable closed-loop attribution and integrated measurement across platforms.
- Agencies can now optimize frequency caps, reduce ad repetition through dynamic creative personalization, and measure sequential storytelling impact.
- Provides a centralized clean room environment controlled by Omnicom, improving data transparency and publisher comparison.

## Why This Matters for My Work

- Provides a scalable, privacy-compliant solution to a perennial challenge in CTV advertising: cross-platform frequency capping.
- Enhances programmatic architectures by integrating identity resolution (Acxiom Real ID), creative-level tracking (Creative ID), and multi-source data fusion in a clean room framework.
- Demonstrates effective partnerships with data infrastructure (Snowflake), retail media, and measurement providers to enable granular and actionable performance insights.
- Improves ability to optimize campaign frequency, minimize wasted ad spend, and protect brand equity by reducing viewer fatigue and negative reach.
- Enables better cross-streamer inventory management and campaign decisioning, critical for SSPs providing transparency and measurement capabilities.
- Clean room approach aligns with privacy regulations by controlling data in a secured, governed environment while supporting measurement continuity.
- Supports advanced tactics like sequential storytelling and dynamic creative optimization by providing detailed frequency and exposure data.
- Sets new standards for how SSPs and advertisers can collaborate to break down walled gardens and deliver improved CTV and linear TV inventory monetization.
